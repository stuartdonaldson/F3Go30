YOUR DAILY MISSION

Each day scores one of three ways: Hit (1) if you did it, Miss (0) if you didn't, or Failed to report (−1) if you forget to check in — so it always pays to report, even a miss.

  1. Choose your challenge — small, specific, actionable (e.g. 15 min stretching, daily journaling).
  2. Record your score — tap "I Hit it!" / "Missed it" each day right here on the check-in page. All Go30 data lives in a shared Google Sheet called the Tracker — you never need to open it, but if you prefer, you can check in by entering 1 for a Hit / 0 for a Miss directly in its Tracker tab, or open it any time from the chart icon (📊) — either way updates the same record.
  3. Beat the deadline — report by 10:00 AM the next day. There's a short grace period, but it expires that same day — after that the system automatically marks it a −1 for you, so don't rely on it.

BONUS POINTS

Beyond your daily challenge, log EHing an FNG, a Fellowship moment, taking a Q, or an Inspire moment for extra points — EHing an FNG counts most. Add these from the Bonus Points section (trophy icon), or directly in the Bonus Tracker sheet. EHing FNG, Q Point, and Inspire need a link to Slack as evidence before they count.

STRENGTH IN NUMBERS

You can go it alone — but you won't go as far. Team up with 3–5 fellow HIMs who share your drive, usually grouped by AO since Shieldlock works best with proximity: hold each other to the standard, call out the misses, celebrate the wins. Joining a team is optional but highly encouraged — you can also participate solo.
