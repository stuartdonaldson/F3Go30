## **What is Go30?** {#what-is-go30?}

Go30 is a monthly habit/goal tracker for the F3 Puget Sound PAX. It helps men accelerate their lives by executing their Daily Challenge aligned with their Ultimate Goals, with accountability from fellow HIMs.

## **What is the Daily Challenge?** {#what-is-the-daily-challenge?}

A small, specific goal aimed at building positive habits or breaking negative ones to achieve your Ultimate Goals.

## **What is the Ultimate Goal?** {#what-is-the-ultimate-goal?}

The larger goal a HIM is working towards to maximize his impact on his community and Concentrica.

## **What Daily Challenge should I choose?** {#what-daily-challenge-should-i-choose?}

Pick something actionable, well-defined, and challenging but sustainable. Examples:

* No phone use while driving (unless on speaker).  
* 15 minutes of stretching daily.  
* No eating after dinner or 8 pm, whichever is earlier.  
* Log all food and stay within 10% of calorie target.  
* Journal daily for 15 minutes or 2 pages.

## **How do I record my daily scores?** {#how-do-i-record-my-daily-scores?}

Use the Go30 Google Sheet (found in the \#go30 channel on Slack). Enter `0` for a miss, `1` for success.

## **I signed up for Go30, now what?** {#i-signed-up-for-go30,-now-what?}

Access the Go30 sheet, act on your goal, and report daily.

## **Why do I have a \-1?** {#why-do-i-have-a--1?}

You didn’t report a `0` or `1` by 10am the following day.

## **How can I get bonus points?** {#how-can-i-get-bonus-points?}

* **Fellowship:** Participate in non-AO activities with Go30 PAX such as an OTB workout or coffeeteria. (1 point/week).  
* **Q a Workout:** Lead a workout (1 point/week).  
* **EH an FNG:** Bring a Sad Clown into F3 (5 points per EH, no limit).  
* **Inspire:** Post something motivational or about your progress or encouraging others in the Go30 channel (1 point/week).

## **How do weekly bonuses work?** {#how-do-weekly-bonuses-work?}

Bonuses are tracked Sunday-Saturday. Occasionally, months align so you can earn extra bonuses. This isn’t a glitch—just a small reward for consistency and effort.

## **Why are bonus points available?** {#why-are-bonus-points-available?}

To encourage fellowship, Qing, EHing, and engagement on the Go30 channel.

## **What do bonus points have to do with my habit changes?** {#what-do-bonus-points-have-to-do-with-my-habit-changes?}

They support your growth by encouraging community engagement, leadership, and outreach.

## **How do I record Bonus Points?** {#how-do-i-record-bonus-points?}

Go to the Bonus Tracker tab and input the required details. Use the pulldown menu for fellowship points.

## **Why aren’t my Bonus Points showing?** {#why-aren’t-my-bonus-points-showing?}

Possible reasons:

* You didn’t log them in the Bonus Tracker.  
* You reached your weekly cap for that type of bonus.  
* Missing required Slack link (Backblast, Go30 post, or EH proof).  
* The Go30 sheet is under construction—contact the Go30 Q via the [Go30 Info and Links canvas page](https://f3pugetsound.slack.com/docs/T78NKT50E/F076NL39880) linked at the top of the [Slack channel](https://app.slack.com/client/T78NKT50E/CBX48QS9X).

## **Why are there teams?** {#why-are-there-teams?}

To provide accountability and encourage fellowship. Shieldlock works best with proximity, so teams are grouped by AOs.

## **How many men can be on a team?** {#how-many-men-can-be-on-a-team?}

Ideally 3-5 for strong accountability, but more or fewer is fine if the team agrees.

## **Can I participate remotely?** {#can-i-participate-remotely?}

Yes.

## **Do I have to join a team?** {#do-i-have-to-join-a-team?}

No, but it's highly encouraged.

## **Can I participate solo?** {#can-i-participate-solo?}

Yes, choose “Solo” for your AO.

## **Why isn’t my AO available on the Go30 sign-up?** {#why-isn’t-my-ao-available-on-the-go30-sign-up?}

Your AO may be new, underrepresented, or not enough PAX joined for a viable team. Spread the word to recruit more members.

## **How do I sign up for Go30?** {#how-do-i-sign-up-for-go30?}

Check Slack near the end of the month. The sign-up sheet circulates about 5 days before the new month. You can sign up anytime during the month. See the [Go30 Info and Links canvas page](https://f3pugetsound.slack.com/docs/T78NKT50E/F076NL39880) linked at the top of the [Slack channel](https://app.slack.com/client/T78NKT50E/CBX48QS9X).

## **What software will I need for Go30?** {#what-software-will-i-need-for-go30?}

None, but having Google Sheets on your phone makes daily check-ins easier.

## **What if I’m out of cell/wi-fi coverage?** {#what-if-i’m-out-of-cell/wi-fi-coverage?}

Arrange with teammates to log scores for you. Change to `0` if you miss a day. This is an honor system.
