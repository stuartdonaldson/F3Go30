## **What is Go30?** {#what-is-go30?}

Go30 is a monthly habit and goal tracker for the F3 Puget Sound PAX. It helps men accelerate their lives by executing a Daily Challenge, tracking progress, and staying accountable with fellow HIMs.

## **What is the Daily Challenge?** {#what-is-the-daily-challenge?}

A Daily Challenge is a small, specific goal aimed at building a positive habit or breaking a negative one.

## **What Daily Challenge should I choose?** {#what-daily-challenge-should-i-choose?}

Pick something actionable, well-defined, and challenging but sustainable.

Examples:

* No phone use while driving unless on speaker.  
* 15 minutes of stretching daily.  
* No eating after dinner or 8pm, whichever is earlier.  
* Log all food and stay within 10% of calorie target.  
* Journal daily for 15 minutes or 2 pages.

A good Daily Challenge should be easy to score as either a `1` or a `0`.

## **How do I record my daily scores?** {#how-do-i-record-my-daily-scores?}

Use the Go30 check-in page (the link is in your confirmation email, or on the Go30 Info and Links canvas page). Enter your F3 Name and email, then tap **I Hit it!** / **Missed it** for today — if yesterday was left blank, it will offer that too.

After you check in, you'll land on your dashboard: streak, total score, weekly bonus points, and your team's board.

You can also check in from the legacy Go30 Tracker sheet — enter `1` for success / `0` for a miss directly in the sheet, and it updates the same tracker. From the check-in page, tap the chart icon (📊) any time to open that spreadsheet directly.

## **I signed up for Go30. Now what?** {#i-signed-up-for-go30.-now-what?}

Act on your Daily Challenge and report your score daily on the Go30 check-in page (or directly in the Tracker sheet, see above).

## **Why do I have a \-1?** {#why-do-i-have-a--1?}

You did not report a `0` or `1` by 10am the following day.

A `-1` does not necessarily mean you failed your Daily Challenge. It means you missed the accountability deadline. There is a short grace period after 10am, but it expires later that same day — after that the system automatically applies the `-1` for you. Don't rely on it; report by 10am the next day.

## **Can I reuse last month's goals?** {#can-i-reuse-last-month's-goals?}

Yes. Open the Go30 signup page and enter your F3 name and email. If you signed up last month, it recognizes you and offers to carry your goals forward — you can confirm and move straight to the end, or make changes.

## **Can I change my signup registration?** {#can-i-change-my-signup-registration?}

Yes. Open the signup page again and enter your F3 name and email — it will find your existing registration and let you update it.

You can use this to update any of your registration information including email address, Team, and Nag selection.

## **How can I get bonus points?** {#how-can-i-get-bonus-points?}

Bonus points are available for F3 engagement beyond your Daily Challenge.

* **Fellowship:** Participate in non-AO activities with Go30 PAX, such as an unpublished OTB workout with other PAX or coffeeteria. 1 point per week.  
* **Q a Workout:** Lead a workout. 1 point per week.  
* **EH an FNG:** Bring a Sad Clown into F3. 5 points per EH, no limit.  
* **Inspire:** Post something motivational, progress-related, or encouraging in the Go30 channel. 1 point per week.

## **How do weekly bonuses work?** {#how-do-weekly-bonuses-work?}

Bonuses are tracked Sunday through Saturday.

Occasionally, months align so you can earn extra bonuses. This is not a glitch. It is a small reward for consistency and effort.

## **Why are bonus points available?** {#why-are-bonus-points-available?}

Bonus points encourage fellowship, Qing, EHing, and engagement in the Go30 channel.

## **What do bonus points have to do with habit change?** {#what-do-bonus-points-have-to-do-with-habit-change?}

Your Daily Challenge is the main habit work.

Bonus points support that work by encouraging community engagement, leadership, and outreach. Go30 works best when PAX are connected and encouraging each other.

## **How do I record Bonus Points?** {#how-do-i-record-bonus-points?}

From the Go30 check-in page (or your dashboard), tap the trophy button to list, add, or edit your own bonus entries — no need to open the spreadsheet.

You can also use the legacy **Bonus Tracker** tab and enter the required details there instead; both update the same tracker. Use the pulldown menu for fellowship points.

## **Why aren’t my Bonus Points showing?** {#why-aren’t-my-bonus-points-showing?}

Possible reasons:

* You did not log them (via the check-in page's bonus section or the Bonus Tracker sheet).  
* You reached the weekly cap for that bonus type.  
* You entered the wrong date, such as the wrong month or wrong year.  
* A required Slack link is missing, such as a Backblast, Go30 post, or EH proof — EHing an FNG, taking a Q, and Inspire all need one before they count.  
* The Go30 sheet is under construction.

For help, contact the Go30 Q through the Go30 Info and Links canvas page linked at the top of the Slack channel.

## **Why are there teams?** {#why-are-there-teams?}

Teams provide accountability and encourage fellowship.

Shieldlock works best with proximity, so teams are usually grouped by AO.

## **How many men can be on a team?** {#how-many-men-can-be-on-a-team?}

Ideally, 3–5 men make a strong accountability team.

More or fewer is fine if the team agrees.

## **Can I participate remotely?** {#can-i-participate-remotely?}

Yes.

## **Do I have to join a team?** {#do-i-have-to-join-a-team?}

No, but it is highly encouraged.

## **Can I participate solo?** {#can-i-participate-solo?}

Yes. Choose **Solo** for your AO.

## **Why isn’t my AO available on the Go30 signup?** {#why-isn’t-my-ao-available-on-the-go30-signup?}

Your AO may be new, underrepresented, or not have enough PAX signed up for a viable team.

You can:

* Spread the word and recruit more PAX from your AO.  
* Choose **Goal based \- or other team** and enter a team name of your choosing.  
* Choose **Solo**.

## **How do I sign up for Go30?** {#how-do-i-sign-up-for-go30?}

Check Slack near the end of the month.

A link to the Go30 signup page usually circulates about 5 days before the new month. You can also sign up anytime during the month.

Open the link, enter your F3 name and email, and follow the steps — if you signed up before, it finds your prior registration and lets you reuse or update it.

See the Go30 Info and Links canvas page linked at the top of the `#go30` channel.

## **What software will I need for Go30?** {#what-software-will-i-need-for-go30?}

Just a phone or computer browser — the signup and check-in pages work on either. Having Google Sheets on your phone makes it easier to check the Tracker sheet directly if you want to.

## **What if I’m out of cell or Wi-Fi coverage?** {#what-if-i’m-out-of-cell-or-wi-fi-coverage?}

Arrange with teammates to log scores for you, on the check-in page or directly in the Tracker sheet.

You can also log in advance and correct it afterward if needed.

If you miss a day, change the score to `0`.

Go30 is an honor system.
