YOUR DAILY MISSION

  1. Choose your challenge — small, specific, actionable (e.g. 15 min stretching, daily journaling).
  2. Record your score in the Go30 Google Sheet in #go30 Slack. Enter 1 for success, 0 for a miss.
  3. Beat the deadline — report by 10:00 AM the next day or receive a −1.

STRENGTH IN NUMBERS

You can go it alone — but you won't go as far. Work with 3–5 fellow HIMs who share your drive: hold each other to the standard, call out the misses, celebrate the wins. Men who pursue hard things together become men who achieve them.
